# Home-VS-Away-EPL

# English Premier League - Home Vs Away. What`s better?

Football is an amazing sport where every detail is important to achieve success. Of course, the success of a team primarily depends on the players, coaches and other members of the team staff, but in this analysis we will find out whether the fans influence the results of the English Premier League teams. And we will test the main stereotype about Newcastle, whether the home stadium really gives them strength and luck.

The data was taken from the English Premier League season starting from 20-21 and ending with the 24-25 season. In addition, teams that had fewer than 10 wins in the selected seasons were removed from the data set (these teams were included in the list of those with fewer than 10 wins due to the fact that they only participated in 1 season of the Premier League).

# Analysis

If we reduce the goal of this analysis to 1 sentence, we can call it this way: "Does the home stadium factor affect the results of teams"). So let's start from the very beginning (all the information written below is taken from the formed dashboard that you saw before this text). In general, from the 20-21 season to the 24-25 season, teams that played at home won more often, in the overall structure, 801 victories of home teams accounted for 630 victories of teams that played away. Thus, in 43.53% of matches, home teams won, and in 34.24% - away teams, in other cases the match ended in a draw. So, we have a certain pattern indicating the truthfulness of the influence of the home stadium on the result of matches. 

But, let's consider additional statistics on teams. In the course of this study, the h/a ratio metric was calculated, which indicates the ratio of home wins to away wins. The higher this indicator, the more victories the team has at home compared to away, and if it is less than 1, then there are more victories away from home. Why did we take this indicator? And in order to track the influence of the home stadium, because taking the number of victories, it is clear that the top 6 teams have more victories, both at home and away from other teams. Now, look at the dashboard, namely the graph of the Best teams at home compared to away. All experts in English football have heard the stereotype about Newcastle, that they play much better at their home stadium. And this turned out to be true, because Newcastle is in 2nd place in our ranking. Newcastle has 1.48 victories at home for every 1 victory away from home. In general, if we look at the top teams by this indicator, we see many teams that are not leaders in the Premier League, which can be explained by the fact that for very strong teams the home stadium factor has a smaller impact.

At the end of our analysis, let's look at the best teams away compared to home games, there you will see that there is no value less than 1, and therefore in our data set there is no team that has more away wins than at home. Let me remind you that teams that had less than 10 wins were removed from the data set.

So, we have determined that the home stadium and fans really have an impact on the team's results. For some teams, the home stadium becomes extremely important (such as Newcastle or Tottenham), but in general for the seasons 20-21 - 24-25 among the teams that played more than 1 season, there is no one that had more away wins than at home. And in general, in 45% of matches the home team won, while in 34% of matches the away team won. For some people it may seem that football players do not pay attention to the fans, and fans are needed only for marketing and profit, but today we found out that this is not so.
